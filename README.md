# BiseoCore

> Your personal AI assistant that knows your context, manages your day, and acts on your behalf.

Built with [NestJS](https://nestjs.com/) · TypeScript · Clean Architecture

---

## Features

### 💬 Communication
- **Chat** — multi-turn conversational AI with persistent memory and multi-model support (Gemini, OpenAI, Anthropic, Ollama)
- **Email Manager** — read, draft, summarize, and reply to emails with AI assistance
- **Notifications** — smart summaries and priority-ranked alerts across all channels

### 🧠 AI Memory & Personalization
- **AI Memory** — learns your habits, preferences, and context over time
- **Contact Manager** — lightweight personal CRM (birthdays, last interaction, notes)

### ✅ Productivity
- **Planner / Task Manager** — create tasks, set deadlines, manage priorities, and track progress (includes habit tracking)
- **Calendar Assistant** — schedule events, find free slots, resolve conflicts, and suggest optimal meeting times
- **Goal Setting** — long-term goals broken into milestones, tracked over weeks and months
- **Note Taking** — capture and organize notes with auto-tagging, linking, and summarization
- **Reminders** — time-based, location-based, and context-aware alerts

### 🔍 Information & Research
- **Web Search** — real-time search with AI-summarized results
- **Document Q&A** — upload PDFs, docs, and images, then ask questions about them
- **Knowledge Base** — personal wiki the AI can read, write, and reference in conversations
- **Bookmarks / Read Later** — save links with automatic AI summarization

### 📋 Daily Assistant
- **Daily Briefing** — morning summary of tasks, calendar, unread emails, and relevant news
- **Journal** — guided journaling with AI-powered reflection and mood tracking
- **Health & Wellness** — log sleep, exercise, and water intake with trend insights

### ⚙️ Automation
- **Workflows** — event-driven actions (e.g. "when I get an email from X, summarize it and create a task")
- **Integrations** — connect to Google Calendar, Notion, Slack, GitHub, and more via plugins
